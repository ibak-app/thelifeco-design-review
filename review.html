00Executive Summary

Bottom Line

Design readiness: 4.5 / 10

The new website design shows a strong structural foundation with good layout and section organization, but has critical gaps that must be addressed before development or public review:

  • Brand identity is missing — the design is fully grayscale with no trace of TheLifeCo's signature teal (#00BED6). After 20 years of brand equity, this needs to be an intentional decision, not an oversight.
  • ~40% of content is placeholder — Lorem ipsum in hero, testimonials, and descriptions. The focus group cannot meaningfully review without real copy.
  • No booking path exists — no "Book Now" in navigation, no pricing, no availability. This is the #1 conversion driver for competitors.
  • Only 3 of ~15 needed page templates are designed (homepage, resort overview, treatment listing). Missing: individual resort pages, booking flow, about, contact, blog.
  • Grammar errors in English copy — "Sign to Webinar", "Terms & Conditionals", "%10", "treatments suits" — these will erode credibility with international guests.

What's Good

  • Clean, modern layout structure with clear visual hierarchy
  • Strong treatment programs section (15 programs, 5 categories, filter UI)
  • Both desktop (1440px) and mobile (360px) variants exist
  • Location-based navigation concept is solid

Key Numbers

3 desktop frames 2 mobile frames 0 published styles 0 components 15 type sizes 16 gray colors 0 accent colors ~40% placeholder text 8+ grammar issues ~15% page coverage

Recommendation

Do not proceed to development yet. Address P0 items (content, brand color, grammar) and have the focus group review with real copy in place. Current state is suitable for layout and structure feedback only.

03UX / UI Audit

Navigation

Only 4 nav items — missing critical links
No About Us, Locations, Blog, Contact, or Booking. Current site has richer navigation. Users from Google/Instagram won't find what they expect.
✔ Add: About, Locations, Programs, Blog, Contact. Keep Retreats/Resorts as sub-items.
No booking CTA in navigation
Current Phuket site has "CALL NOW" in header. The new design has no persistent booking button. This is the #1 conversion killer for wellness websites.
✔ Add sticky "Book Now" or "Get a Free Consultation" in header.

Hero Section

"Own Your Future" — too vague for wellness
Could be finance, tech, or education. Doesn't communicate wellness, healing, or transformation. Competitors: "Transformative Wellness Retreat" (Phuket site), "Haven of Life" (SHA).
✔ Consider: "Transform Your Health. Transform Your Life." / "Where Healing Meets Luxury"
Lorem ipsum hero subtitle
The most important paragraph on the site is placeholder text. Should convey TheLifeCo's core value prop in 1-2 sentences.
✔ "Medically supervised wellness retreats in Turkey, Thailand, and the Caribbean. 20 years of expertise. 30,000+ guests transformed."

Locations

Location inconsistencies
Frame 01: Turkey, Thailand, St. Lucia, Greece. Frame 02 adds Egypt. Mykonos (Greece) shown but isn't a real TLC location. Sharm El Sheikh appears in footer but not in tabs.
✔ Verify actual operating locations. Remove Mykonos if not real.

Testimonials

Fake testimonials + wrong stats
All quotes are Lorem ipsum attributed to "TheGuardian". Stat says "10,000+" — real number is 30,000+. TLC has real testimonials from guests on their existing site and Tripadvisor.
✔ Use real quotes from thelifeco.com/testimonials and actual 30,000+ stat.

Treatment Programs

Strong treatment listing — 15 programs, 5 categories, filter UI
Well-organized with Programs/Conditions/Sort filters. This is the strongest section in the design.
No descriptions, durations, or pricing on cards
Each card only shows name + category. Users need: brief description, duration range, and starting price.
✔ Add 1-line description + "from X days / from $X" to each card.

07Wording & Grammar Issues

"Sign to Webinar"
Should be "Sign Up for Webinar" or "Register for Webinar"
"Terms & Conditionals"
Should be "Terms & Conditions" — "Conditionals" is a grammar concept, not legal
"Earn %10 of your purchase"
English: "10%" not "%10" — Turkish convention used
"Lets discover which treatments suits you best"
Two errors: "Let's" (needs apostrophe) + "treatments suit" (subject-verb agreement)
Inconsistent CTA capitalization
"Talk to an expert" vs "Talk To An Expert" — pick one style
"Retreatments" in footer
Not a standard English word. If intentional portmanteau, needs introduction. If typo, fix to "Treatments".
"© 2024 Powered by LifeCo"
Year: 2026. "Powered by" implies platform. Should be "© 2026 TheLifeCo Wellbeing"
Treatment program names — excellent
All 15 names are professional, descriptive, and search-friendly.
Phuket resort description — authentic and compelling
"Phuket's Best Detox Center with Unique Programs" — specific and matches brand voice.

08Accessibility

Multiple grays fail WCAG AA contrast on white
#919191 on white = 3.0:1 (fail). #848484 = 3.5:1 (fail). Need #666 or darker for body text (6.0:1).
No focus/hover states designed
Keyboard users cannot navigate. No visible focus indicators on buttons or links.
Icon-only links without text alternatives
arrow-up-right, angle-right/left icons have no accessible labels for screen readers.
