Program Overview

TheLifeCo's Diabetes Recovery is a comprehensive, doctor-led programme that uses rigorously evidence-based nutritional and lifestyle interventions to support metabolic health and diabetes management. Combining continuous glucose monitoring with plant-based nutrition, structured exercise therapy, and stress management, the programme addresses the multiple factors that drive blood sugar dysregulation.

Many guests observe significant improvements in fasting blood glucose levels within the first week of the programme. HbA1c testing at programme start and conclusion allows us to document meaningful metabolic change, and our endocrinologists work alongside you to adjust any medications in coordination with your primary care physician where appropriate.

Personalised protocols are developed based on whether you are managing Type 1 or Type 2 diabetes, your current medication regimen, and your individual metabolic profile. You leave with a detailed home management plan and benefit from a structured 3-month follow-up programme.

This programme supports and complements — but does not replace — your existing medical care. Any medication adjustments are made only in coordination with your treating physician. Please consult your doctor before booking and bring your full medication list and recent blood work to your arrival consultation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Important questions answered about the Diabetes Recovery medical programme.

Yes, we accept guests with both Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes, and we develop entirely separate personalised protocols for each. Type 2 diabetes has significantly greater reversibility potential through lifestyle intervention, and many guests with Type 2 achieve substantial reductions in HbA1c and medication dependence. For Type 1 guests, our focus is on improved blood sugar stability, reduced variability, and enhanced quality of life through better nutritional and lifestyle strategies.
Any medication adjustments are made only by our endocrinologists in coordination with your treating physician back home, and always with your full knowledge and consent. As blood glucose improves through the programme, reductions in certain medications may become appropriate under medical guidance — but we never make changes without transparent communication and proper safety protocols in place. Bring your full medication list and your most recent blood test results to your arrival consultation.
Our 3-month remote follow-up includes monthly video consultations with your assigned doctor, ongoing access to our nutritionist via messaging, a structured eating and exercise framework tailored for your home environment, and a final 3-month HbA1c re-test recommendation for you to complete with your local GP. This follow-up period is critical for consolidating the metabolic improvements achieved during your stay and ensuring sustainable long-term results.
Many guests with Type 2 diabetes observe measurable improvements in fasting glucose within the first 5–7 days of the programme, primarily driven by the shift to a low-glycaemic plant-based nutrition protocol and the introduction of daily structured exercise. Continuous glucose monitoring allows us to track your response in real time and adjust your programme accordingly. Results vary based on individual metabolic status, programme duration, medication use, and adherence to the protocols.
