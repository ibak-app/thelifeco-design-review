True Beauty Begins From Within

TheLifeCo's Beauty and Glow Program takes a dual approach to radiance — nourishing the body from the inside while enhancing it professionally from the outside. Anti-inflammatory raw vegan nutrition floods your cells with antioxidants, vitamins, and enzymes that directly influence skin clarity, elasticity, and luminosity. The impact on skin tone and texture becomes visible as early as day three of the program.

Alongside the nutritional foundation, daily professional spa treatments address the external layer: anti-aging facial therapies using advanced serums and techniques, body wraps formulated to contour and hydrate, and lymphatic drainage massage to reduce puffiness and improve circulation. Hydrotherapy sessions stimulate collagen synthesis and oxygenate the skin.

A tailored beauty supplement protocol, hair and scalp treatments, and daily yoga complete the program — leaving guests with a lasting glow that goes far deeper than the surface.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most guests notice a visible improvement in skin clarity and brightness from day three of the program, as the combination of eliminated processed foods, increased hydration, and antioxidant nutrition begins to reduce inflammation and oxidative stress. By the end of a 7-day program, the majority of guests report visibly clearer, more even, and more luminous skin. 14-day program guests often describe the results as transformative.
The daily facial treatment is personalized during your initial skin analysis consultation. Options include deep cleansing and purifying treatments for congested skin, hyaluronic acid infusion for dehydrated skin, vitamin C brightening facials for uneven tone, and collagen-stimulating treatments using micro-current or LED therapy. Your aesthetician will build a progression throughout your stay, starting with deep cleansing and advancing to active anti-aging treatments as your skin responds.
The Beauty and Glow Program is one of our most popular gift choices. It does not involve fasting or any intensive medical protocols, making it highly accessible and enjoyable for first-time guests. Gift reservations can be made through our booking team, who will work with the recipient directly to choose preferred dates, destination, and program duration. Gift certificates are available with an open validity period of 12 months.
Sustainability of results depends largely on dietary habits maintained after departure. Every guest leaves with a beauty nutrition guide, a recommended supplement protocol, and home skincare recommendations curated by your aesthetician. Guests who maintain a predominantly plant-rich diet and the key supplement protocol consistently report that their improved complexion persists and even continues to improve for several weeks after the program ends.
