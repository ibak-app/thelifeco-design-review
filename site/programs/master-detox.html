Program Overview

TheLifeCo's Master Detox is our signature and most sought-after program — a comprehensive medically supervised detoxification combining raw vegan nutrition, targeted supplement protocols, colon hydrotherapy, and an array of holistic therapies.

Designed to eliminate accumulated toxins from every organ system, the program systematically resets metabolism, restores optimal liver and gut function, and re-establishes healthy cellular communication throughout the body. Every protocol is personalised to your health history and goals.

Daily medical monitoring, comprehensive blood work, and continuous personalised adjustments ensure your body detoxifies safely and efficiently under full doctor supervision — so you can trust every step of the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the Master Detox program.

The Master Detox is suitable for most healthy adults seeking a deep cleanse, metabolic reset, or relief from chronic low energy, digestive issues, or skin problems. An initial medical consultation determines your suitability and allows our doctors to personalise your protocol. Guests with serious medical conditions should consult their GP prior to booking.
We offer the programme in 7, 14, and 21-day formats. For first-time guests, 7 days provides a meaningful reset and gives you a safe introduction to the detox process. Returning guests or those with more intensive health goals often choose 14 or 21 days for deeper cellular cleansing and sustained results. Our medical team will recommend the optimal duration based on your health assessment.
The Master Detox is not a starvation diet. You will be consuming raw vegan meals, fresh juices, herbal teas, and a comprehensive supplement protocol — so hunger is not a significant concern for most guests. Some guests experience mild detox symptoms such as headaches or fatigue in the first 1–3 days as the body begins releasing stored toxins. These are normal and closely monitored by our medical team, who adjust your protocol as needed.
Before departure, you will receive a detailed home protocol from your doctor outlining how to maintain and build on your results. This includes a re-introduction eating plan, supplement recommendations, and lifestyle guidance. We also offer post-programme support via our wellness team. Many guests return annually to continue their health journey.
