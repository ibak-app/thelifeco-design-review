Program Overview

The Green Detox is a vibrant, nutrient-rich cleanse built around cold-pressed green juices, raw vegan superfood meals, and carefully selected gentle detox therapies. It is the ideal choice for first-time guests or anyone seeking a more accessible entry point into the world of therapeutic cleansing.

Rather than restriction, the Green Detox is about abundance — flooding the body with an extraordinary concentration of vitamins, minerals, enzymes, and antioxidants while simultaneously giving the digestive system the rest it needs to repair, reset, and thrive. The result is a profound improvement in energy, clarity, and overall well-being.

Our raw food workshops are a signature component — guests leave with practical knowledge and recipes to continue building healthy habits at home, extending the benefits of their programme far beyond the resort.

What's Included

A complete nutrient-loading cleanse experience with education and practical tools to maintain your results.

  • Cold-pressed green juices — 6 servings per day
  • Raw vegan superfood meals crafted by our nutrition team
  • Medical consultation and health assessment on arrival
  • Targeted supplement protocol to support detoxification
  • Daily yoga and movement classes
  • Raw food cooking workshop with our head chef
  • Herbal tea selection available throughout the day
  • Wellness lectures on nutrition, gut health, and sustainable eating

Key Benefits

Guests on the Green Detox consistently experience these meaningful health improvements.

Nutrient Flooding

Your cells receive a concentrated infusion of micronutrients from whole plant sources that most diets cannot provide.

Gentle Detoxification

Digestion is rested while the liver and lymphatic system are actively supported — a thorough yet comfortable cleanse.

Healthy Habit Building

Practical workshops and nutrition education mean you leave with skills and habits to sustain your improvements at home.

Glowing Skin

Antioxidant flooding and digestive rest translate directly to clearer, brighter skin — often visible within days.
