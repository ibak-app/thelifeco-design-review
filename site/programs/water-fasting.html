Program Overview

TheLifeCo's Water Fasting program is one of the most advanced therapeutic fasting experiences available — conducted under constant medical supervision with daily check-ups, blood monitoring, and precise electrolyte management to ensure complete safety at every stage.

The body enters autophagy — the process of recycling and clearing damaged cells — after approximately 24–72 hours of fasting. Extended fasting then triggers stem cell regeneration, delivering cellular renewal that simply cannot be achieved through other means. The science behind therapeutic fasting is well-documented and continues to attract significant research interest.

A carefully guided re-feeding protocol at the conclusion of your programme ensures a safe transition back to eating, protecting the benefits you have built and preparing your body for long-term healthy nutrition.

Key Benefits

Water fasting delivers profound physiological benefits that are unique to extended therapeutic fasting.

Cellular Autophagy

The body's natural self-cleaning process is activated, clearing damaged proteins and cellular debris for true regeneration.

Stem Cell Regeneration

Extended fasting triggers the production of new stem cells, supporting immune system renewal and tissue repair.

Metabolic Reset

Insulin sensitivity is dramatically improved and metabolic flexibility restored, creating a foundation for long-term health.

Mental Clarity

Many guests report profound mental clarity, heightened focus, and emotional calm during and after therapeutic fasting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Important questions answered about our medically supervised Water Fasting program.

When conducted under proper medical supervision, therapeutic water fasting is safe for healthy adults. At TheLifeCo, every guest is evaluated by our doctors before beginning the programme. We monitor vital signs and blood parameters daily, manage electrolyte balance, and are prepared to modify or stop the programme at any point if needed. We do not recommend water fasting for guests with certain medical conditions, and our pre-programme assessment ensures suitability.
Autophagy is the body's cellular housekeeping process — it breaks down and recycles damaged proteins, dysfunctional organelles, and cellular waste. It was the subject of the 2016 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine. Triggering autophagy through fasting is associated with reduced disease risk, slowed aging, improved brain function, and enhanced immune health. Meaningful autophagy typically begins 24–48 hours into a water fast.
Your days are structured around gentle movement, rest, and mind-body practices appropriate for fasting. You will attend daily gentle yoga, guided meditation, and wellness lectures. Many guests use the quiet time for journaling, reading, or simply resting in our peaceful resort environments. You will have regular doctor check-ins and access to our wellness team throughout the day.
The Master Detox is our recommended starting point for most guests — it includes food (raw vegan meals and juices) and provides a comprehensive cleanse with less intensity. Water Fasting is typically chosen by guests who have prior detox experience, are seeking maximal cellular regeneration, or have specific health goals around autophagy and metabolic reset. Our wellness consultants will help you choose the right programme during your free pre-booking consultation.
