Program Overview

The Juice Cleanse is an intensive juice-only experience for rapid, focused detoxification. Throughout each day you will receive 6–8 freshly pressed juices combining fruits, vegetables, and superfoods — each formulated to deliver maximum nutritional benefit while giving your digestive system a complete rest.

With durations of 3–7 days, the Juice Cleanse is perfect as a weekend reset between longer programmes, an introduction to TheLifeCo's cleansing philosophy, or a targeted intervention to kickstart weight loss or break unhealthy eating patterns.

Our nutritionists customise your daily juice selection based on your individual health goals — whether that is supporting liver detox, boosting immunity, reducing inflammation, or energising your system — so every glass is working purposefully for your body.

What's Included

Everything needed for a focused, expertly guided juice-only cleanse from arrival to departure.

  • 6–8 freshly pressed juices daily, customised for your goals
  • Nutritionist consultation to personalise your juice protocol
  • Custom juice recipe card set based on your health profile
  • Curated herbal tea selection available throughout the day
  • Light yoga sessions adapted for juice cleanse participants
  • Wellness orientation and daily programme check-in
  • Take-home juice recipe booklet for continued cleansing at home

Key Benefits

Even a short juice cleanse delivers meaningful physiological benefits and a powerful reset.

Quick Body Reset

Three to seven days is sufficient to clear accumulated dietary residue and reset your body's baseline appetite signals.

Digestive Rest

Removing solid food gives the gut a complete rest, allowing the intestinal lining to repair and inflammation to reduce.

Vitamin Boost

Fresh juice delivers vitamins and minerals in a highly bioavailable form, rapidly replenishing nutrient stores.

Weight Kick-Start

The cleanse reduces water retention, reduces bloating, and sets the metabolic conditions for healthy sustained weight loss.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from guests considering the Juice Cleanse program.

You will receive between 6 and 8 juices per day depending on your programme duration and individual protocol. Juices are served at approximately 2–2.5 hour intervals throughout the day, starting with a morning energising blend and finishing with a calming evening juice. Each juice is designed to serve a specific function — from liver support to immune boosting — at the optimal time of day.
Light to moderate exercise is encouraged and included in the programme through daily gentle yoga sessions. High-intensity or strenuous exercise is not recommended during a juice-only programme as your caloric intake is reduced. Walking, stretching, and light swimming are all appropriate. Our team will guide you on appropriate activity levels based on how you are feeling each day.
Some guests experience mild hunger, particularly in the first 24 hours, as the body adjusts to liquid-only nutrition. This typically passes within a day as blood sugar stabilises and the body adapts. The regular juice intervals are designed to maintain steady energy and satiety. Our nutritionists are available to adjust your protocol if you experience persistent discomfort.
We recommend reducing caffeine, alcohol, processed foods, and animal products for 2–3 days before your arrival to prepare your body and make the transition easier. After the cleanse, we provide a written re-introduction guide recommending that you begin with raw fruits and vegetables before progressing to cooked whole foods over 2–3 days. This protects your results and prevents digestive discomfort from reintroducing solid food too quickly.
