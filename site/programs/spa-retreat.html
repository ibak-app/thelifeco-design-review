Pure Rest. Pure Luxury. Pure Rejuvenation.

TheLifeCo's Spa Retreat is designed for guests who seek complete rest and rejuvenation without committing to a structured detox or intensive wellness program. It is the ideal entry point into the TheLifeCo experience — and equally perfect as a wind-down after a more intensive program or as a thoughtful gift for someone you love.

Each day features a personally selected spa treatment from our full menu — including Swedish, deep tissue, Thai, or hot stone massage; classic or anti-aging facials; and full-body wraps. Gentle morning yoga and a guided afternoon meditation anchor the day in calm, while healthy gourmet meals crafted by our nutrition team nourish without restriction.

Full access to pools, beaches, and resort facilities completes an experience that feels equal parts indulgent and restorative. An evening herbal tea ceremony marks each day's unhurried close.

What's Included

Everything you need to completely unwind — and nothing you don't want.

  • Welcome massage on arrival
  • Daily spa treatment (massage, facial, or body wrap)
