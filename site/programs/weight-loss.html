A Science-Backed Approach to Sustainable Weight Loss

TheLifeCo's Weight Loss Program is a medically supervised weight management protocol combining nutrition science, exercise therapy, and behavioral change techniques. Using advanced body composition analysis and metabolic testing, our physicians create a personalized caloric target and macronutrient profile designed specifically for your physiology.

The program integrates raw vegan nutrition — rich in enzymes, fiber, and micronutrients — with daily structured physical activity ranging from aqua aerobics and yoga to functional strength training. Mindfulness training and behavioral coaching address the psychological dimensions of eating, ensuring the results you achieve during your stay translate into lasting habits at home.

Many guests lose between 3 and 8 kg during their stay, with continued results tracked through our comprehensive home maintenance plan and follow-up consultations.
