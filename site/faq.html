General

About TheLifeCo and our wellness philosophy

6 questions
TheLifeCo is a world-leading wellness and medical retreat company founded in 2005. With five luxury resort destinations spanning Turkey (Bodrum and Antalya), Thailand (Phuket), Egypt (Sharm El Sheikh), and the Caribbean (St. Lucia), we have helped over 30,000 guests from 80+ countries transform their health. Our approach is rooted in the Four Pillars of wellness — nutrition, physical activity, mental wellness, and inner peace — delivered through medically supervised programs designed by a multidisciplinary team of doctors, nutritionists, therapists, and wellness coaches.
We operate five resort destinations worldwide. Bodrum and Antalya are located on Turkey's stunning Aegean and Mediterranean coasts respectively. Phuket sits in southern Thailand amidst lush tropical scenery. Sharm El Sheikh offers year-round warmth on Egypt's Red Sea. St. Lucia is our newest destination, set in the Caribbean's paradise surroundings. Each location has a distinct character and specialisation — our consultants are happy to recommend the right fit for your goals.

Our longevity reflects the quality of our outcomes and the trust of our guest community. We have received numerous international accolades, including the World Spa Awards and consistent recognition from Tatler, Condé Nast Traveller, Forbes, and Vogue.

Programs

Our wellness and medical programs explained

6 questions
We offer over 20 distinct programs, organised into five main categories. Detox programs include the Master Detox, Green Detox, and Raw Food Detox. Fasting programs include supervised Water Fasting (1 to 21 days) and Intermittent Fasting protocols. Medical programs include Diabetes Recovery, Cardiovascular Health, Chronic Inflammation, and Autoimmune Support. Wellness programs include Mental Wellness, Longevity Reset, Beauty & Glow, and Weight Management. Fitness programs include Yoga Immersion and Body Transformation. Programs can be combined and are always personalised.
The Master Detox is our signature program and the most popular entry point for new guests. It combines structured cold-pressed juice protocols, targeted nutritional supplements, and a full schedule of detoxifying therapies — including colon hydrotherapy, lymphatic drainage massage, infrared sauna, and therapeutic bodywork. Daily one-on-one consultations with our medical team monitor your progress and adjust the protocol as needed. Most guests experience significant improvements in energy, digestion, and mental clarity within the first three to four days.
We recommend a minimum of 7 days to experience meaningful physiological change. The first two to three days are typically an adjustment period. Days four through seven is where most guests begin feeling tangible benefits. For deeper cellular repair, chronic condition management, or significant weight goals, a 14 to 21-day stay allows the body time to complete full detoxification cycles, establish new metabolic patterns, and embed lasting behavioural change. We are happy to advise on optimal duration after reviewing your health questionnaire.
Yes — and this is the norm rather than the exception at TheLifeCo. Because your program is fully personalised, your daily schedule is designed to draw from multiple disciplines simultaneously. A guest focusing on weight management might combine elements of detox, supervised fasting, fitness training, and nutritional coaching in a single stay. Our medical team orchestrates the combination, ensuring the interventions complement rather than conflict with each other, and adjusts the balance as your body responds.
Our nutritional philosophy is centred on raw, organic, plant-based food. Depending on your program, this ranges from cold-pressed juices and smoothies (during detox and fasting programs) to beautifully prepared raw-vegan meals featuring salads, nut-based dishes, dehydrated breads, and raw-food sushi. All produce is organic, locally sourced where possible, and prepared by chefs trained in plant-based cuisine. Guests frequently tell us they are surprised by how satisfying and varied the food is — many leave with new favourite dishes they continue preparing at home.
We welcome guests at all levels. If you have no prior wellness experience, our team will guide you from day one with clarity, patience, and genuine care. Our pre-arrival medical consultation allows us to understand your baseline and starting comfort level so we can calibrate the program appropriately. Many guests arrive sceptical and leave transformed — both physically and in their relationship to their own health and lifestyle.

Booking

Reservations, pricing, and logistics

6 questions
You can book through multiple channels: online via our booking page, by emailing info@thelifeco.com, by calling +90 252 413 44 10, or via WhatsApp. We recommend a brief call with one of our wellness consultants before finalising — this conversation helps us understand your goals and ensure we recommend the right program, location, and duration. There is no obligation to book during this consultation.
Your program price includes: accommodation in a wellness-oriented room or suite; all meals and juices aligned to your program (organic, raw-vegan); your full daily program schedule of group sessions; daily medical consultations; core treatment sessions specified in your program (massage, hydrotherapy, colonics); access to all resort facilities including pools and relaxation areas; and a pre-departure consultation with aftercare recommendations. Optional add-ons such as aesthetic therapies, private yoga, or extended spa treatments are priced separately.
Cancellations made 30 or more days before arrival receive a full refund of any deposit paid. Cancellations made 14 to 29 days before arrival receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 14 days of arrival are non-refundable, though we will do our best to accommodate rescheduling requests where possible. We strongly recommend travel insurance that covers medical cancellation, and we are happy to provide supporting documentation for insurance claims.
Yes. For groups of four or more guests booking simultaneously, we offer preferential pricing and can arrange complementary group programming alongside individual sessions. Corporate wellness groups, retreats for friend groups, and family bookings all qualify. Please contact our corporate wellness team directly to discuss bespoke group packages, which can include dedicated group activities, private welcome dinners, and tailored program combinations.
We recommend booking at least 4 to 8 weeks in advance to secure your preferred dates, accommodation type, and program. Peak periods — particularly July, August, and the Christmas–New Year window — can fill several months ahead. Last-minute availability does exist occasionally, particularly at shoulder seasons, but we cannot guarantee specific programs or room categories for late bookings. The earlier you book, the more time our medical team has to review your health questionnaire and prepare a fully personalised protocol.
Yes. For programs of 14 days or longer, we offer structured payment plans that allow you to spread the cost over two to four monthly instalments. Terms vary by program and location. Please contact our reservations team to discuss the options available for your chosen retreat. We want financial planning to be straightforward so you can focus entirely on your health transformation.

Medical

Health, safety, and clinical supervision

5 questions
Yes — unconditionally. All TheLifeCo locations operate with 24/7 on-site medical supervision. Our resident physicians are available at all times for consultations, monitoring, and emergencies. This is a fundamental distinction between TheLifeCo and non-medical wellness retreats. Your safety is never compromised, and the presence of qualified physicians enables us to offer advanced programs — including extended water fasting and therapeutic interventions — that cannot be offered responsibly without continuous medical oversight.
Upon arrival, every guest undergoes a comprehensive medical assessment including: a full blood panel (CBC, metabolic panel, liver and kidney function, lipid profile, glucose, thyroid); body composition analysis; blood pressure and vital signs; and a detailed face-to-face consultation with our physician. These baseline results guide the personalisation of your program. A follow-up assessment at the end of your stay quantifies the improvements achieved, giving you measurable evidence of your transformation.
Yes, and this is one of our most impactful areas of work. We have dedicated programs for Type 2 diabetes recovery, cardiovascular risk reduction, chronic inflammation, metabolic syndrome, autoimmune support, and chronic fatigue, among others. If you have a chronic condition, our team conducts a detailed pre-arrival review to confirm we can support you safely and effectively. In some cases, we may request additional records or specialist clearance — always in your best interests.
Yes, fully. All physicians at TheLifeCo are licensed medical doctors qualified in their home countries and experienced in integrative and lifestyle medicine. Our medical team includes internists, endocrinologists, nutritional medicine specialists, and general practitioners — each with dedicated training in wellness and therapeutic medicine. We hold multiple international accreditations that validate our clinical standards, and our protocols are reviewed regularly by our medical advisory board.
It is normal to experience mild adjustment symptoms in the first one to three days of a detox program — low energy, mild headaches, or digestive changes are common as the body transitions. These are monitored and managed by our medical team as part of your daily consultation. If anything more significant arises, our on-site physicians are available around the clock. In the rare event of a medical emergency requiring hospital-level care, we maintain relationships with local hospitals and will arrange immediate transfer with a member of our team accompanying you.

Resorts

Our locations and what to expect on-site

5 questions
Each location has a distinct character. Bodrum is our adults-only flagship on the Aegean coast — ideal for a calm, focused experience in a stunning Mediterranean setting. Antalya has the largest medical department, best for guests with specific chronic health goals. Phuket offers lush tropical surroundings with a particularly strong yoga, meditation, and mindfulness offering — popular for mental and emotional renewal. Sharm El Sheikh provides year-round warmth in a Red Sea desert setting. St. Lucia is our newest Caribbean destination, combining island luxury with a full wellness program. Our consultants recommend based on your priorities.
Our Bodrum resort is adults-only, creating a particularly calm and focused atmosphere ideal for those seeking deep detox and undistracted rest. Our Antalya, Phuket, Sharm El Sheikh, and St. Lucia resorts welcome families, with programs that can be tailored for parents and older children separately. If you are travelling as a couple seeking maximum tranquillity, Bodrum is our strongest recommendation. If you are bringing family, our consultants will help you choose the most suitable alternative location.
All TheLifeCo resorts feature: temperature-controlled pools and relaxation areas; yoga and meditation studios; treatment rooms for massage, hydrotherapy, and specialist therapies; medical consultation suites; organic restaurant and juice bar; garden or nature spaces for outdoor practice; and a fully equipped wellness centre. Rooms are designed with rest and recovery in mind — spacious, naturally lit, and intentionally calm. Televisions are not included in rooms, in keeping with our digital detox philosophy.
Yes, Wi-Fi is available throughout all our resorts. However, we warmly encourage guests to treat their stay as an opportunity for a genuine digital detox. Reducing screen time, disconnecting from social media, and allowing the mind to truly rest is an important part of the mental wellness dimension of our programs. Many guests report that the enforced separation from constant digital input is one of the most surprising and valuable aspects of their retreat. You are, of course, entirely free to use Wi-Fi as needed.
All food served at TheLifeCo is already 100% plant-based, organic, and free from gluten, dairy, and refined sugars — so many common dietary requirements are already accommodated by default. If you have specific allergies (such as nuts or seeds), please declare them during booking and on your health questionnaire. Our kitchen team will ensure your meals are safe, varied, and delicious. We have supported guests with coeliac disease, severe nut allergies, and a wide range of other dietary needs without compromise to the program experience.
