Your Questions, Answered

A detox program is a medically supervised protocol designed to help your body eliminate accumulated toxins and restore optimal cellular function. Our programs combine precision nutrition, therapeutic fasting, targeted supplementation, and holistic therapies for comprehensive whole-body cleansing and renewal.
Program duration depends on your health goals, current condition, and the depth of transformation you are seeking. Most programs range from 7 to 21 days. Our medical team will assess your individual needs and recommend the optimal duration during your pre-arrival consultation. First-time guests often start with 7–10 days.
Yes. All programs at TheLifeCo include full medical supervision. You will receive a comprehensive health assessment on arrival, regular check-ups, blood work analysis, and daily access to our medical team throughout your stay. Our doctors and wellness specialists monitor your progress and adjust your protocol as needed.
Many guests combine elements from multiple programs during their stay. Our medical team will create a fully personalised protocol that addresses all of your health goals. For example, a detox program can be combined with mental wellness therapies, longevity treatments, or specific chronic disease support modules.
We provide all wellness amenities including robes, slippers, and toiletries. We recommend bringing comfortable, breathable clothing for yoga and movement sessions, any prescribed medications you are currently taking, and your medical records or recent blood test results for the initial consultation. Swimwear is also recommended for hydrotherapy.
Yes. We have dedicated programs for a range of chronic conditions including Type 2 diabetes, cardiovascular disease, chronic fatigue, chronic inflammation, and cancer support. All programs are individually adapted by our medical team to ensure they are safe and appropriate for your specific health situation. We recommend a pre-arrival consultation before booking.
