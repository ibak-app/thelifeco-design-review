Our Flagship Retreat

Aegean Serenity on Göltürkbükü Bay

Tucked away in lush botanical gardens on the shores of Göltürkbükü Bay, TheLifeCo Bodrum is the original home of our wellness philosophy. The Bodrum Peninsula's pristine Aegean waters provide the perfect backdrop for deep transformation, and our adults-only environment ensures complete peace throughout your stay.

The resort's botanical gardens are home to orange, palm, and mandarin trees, creating a living pharmacy of natural wellness. Our private beach offers direct access to the crystal-clear Aegean, while our thermal therapies — including a traditional Turkish hammam, ozone sauna, and salt room — draw on centuries of healing tradition.

Red light therapy and BioCharger sessions complement our comprehensive medical programs, and the raw vegan restaurant and juice bar ensure every meal nourishes cellular regeneration. TheLifeCo Bodrum has welcomed guests from over 80 countries since 2005 and remains our most celebrated retreat.

Life at TheLifeCo Bodrum

Travel

Getting to Bodrum

Bodrum-Milas Airport (BJV)

Fly directly into Bodrum-Milas Airport. International connections via Istanbul (IST or SAW) on Turkish Airlines, Pegasus, and seasonal direct European routes.

45-Minute Airport Transfer

Our team arranges private transfers directly to the resort. The scenic drive through the Bodrum Peninsula takes approximately 45 minutes.

Ferry from Greek Islands

Seasonal ferry connections from Kos, Rhodes, and other Aegean islands make Bodrum easily accessible for guests arriving from Greece.

Planning Your Journey

Our dedicated guest relations team will assist you with all travel arrangements, including airport transfers, visa information for Turkish entry, and any special requirements for your program. We recommend arriving the evening before your program starts to settle in and attend our welcome orientation.

Bodrum experiences warm, dry summers (May–October) and mild winters, making it a year-round destination. Peak wellness season is April through November.
