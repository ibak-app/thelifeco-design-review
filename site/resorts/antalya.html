Clinical Precision on the Turkish Riviera

Located within the 5-star Akra Antalya Hotel on the spectacular Turkish Riviera, TheLifeCo Antalya brings together the very best in luxury hospitality and advanced medical wellness. Panoramic views of the Mediterranean set the scene for a deeply transformative stay.

What distinguishes Antalya is our fully equipped on-site medical center, complete with advanced diagnostic equipment, a dedicated laboratory, and an IV therapy suite staffed by experienced physicians and nurses. Guests receive comprehensive health assessments and fully personalised program protocols based on clinical findings.

Antalya is also a gateway to the ancient Lycian coast and historic sites of southern Turkey, offering guests the opportunity to combine wellness with cultural discovery. The Mediterranean climate is warm and inviting year-round, making this an exceptional destination for any season.

Travel

Getting to Antalya

Antalya Airport (AYT)

Antalya Airport is one of Turkey's busiest international hubs, with direct flights from over 100 cities across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia.

25-Minute Airport Transfer

Private transfers are arranged by our team. The Akra Antalya Hotel is just 25 minutes from the airport along the scenic coastal road.

City Center Access

The resort is close to Antalya's historic Kaleiçi district, the old city harbor, and the ancient ruins of Perge and Aspendos.

Planning Your Journey

Antalya enjoys over 300 days of sunshine per year and warm Mediterranean temperatures from April through November. The mild winter climate makes it a popular retreat for guests seeking year-round wellness in a comfortable setting.

Our medical team recommends arriving at least one day before your program begins to complete your initial health assessment and allow time for laboratory results before your personalized protocol is finalized.
