Getting to St. Lucia

Hewanorra International Airport (UVF)

Saint Lucia's main international airport receives direct flights from London Gatwick, New York JFK, Toronto, and major US hubs via British Airways, Virgin Atlantic, American Airlines, and Air Canada.

45-Minute Transfer to Gros-Islet

Our private transfer service will collect you from Hewanorra and bring you along Saint Lucia's scenic West Coast road to Mount Pimard in Gros-Islet — approximately 45 minutes.

Helicopter Transfer Available

For guests arriving at George F.L. Charles Airport (SLU) in Castries, or those preferring a scenic aerial approach, helicopter transfers can be arranged on request.

Planning Your Journey

Saint Lucia enjoys a tropical climate with warm temperatures year-round, ranging from 24°C to 31°C. The dry season (January–April) offers the most reliably sunny weather, while the lush green season (June–November) brings occasional rain showers that keep the volcanic landscape spectacular.

British, American, Canadian, and EU passport holders do not require a visa to enter Saint Lucia. The island is served by direct flights from London, New York, Toronto, and several other major cities, making it one of the most accessible Caribbean destinations. Our concierge team will assist with all pre-arrival planning.
