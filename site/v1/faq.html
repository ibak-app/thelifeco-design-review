Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about our programs, resorts, and how to prepare for your wellness journey.

TheLifeCo was founded in 2005 with a simple but powerful mission: to help people reclaim their health through science-based, medically supervised wellness retreats. Over the past two decades, we have welcomed guests from more than 60 countries across our resort centers in Turkey (Bodrum and Antalya), Thailand (Phuket), Egypt (Sharm El Sheikh), and the Caribbean (St. Lucia).

We combine integrative medicine, functional nutrition, detox protocols, and holistic therapies to create personalized programs that address the root causes of health challenges — not just symptoms.

Unlike spa resorts or lifestyle retreats, TheLifeCo operates under full medical supervision. Every guest completes a comprehensive health assessment upon arrival, and their program is overseen by a team of doctors, nutritionists, and wellness specialists. Our protocols are grounded in clinical research — particularly around fasting, detoxification, and metabolic health — and we maintain strict safety standards at every location.

Our 20-year track record and consistent 5-star guest reviews reflect a commitment to genuine, measurable health outcomes.

Each resort offers a unique setting and atmosphere while delivering the same high standard of care. Bodrum sits above the Aegean sea with a sophisticated, modern facility — ideal for guests seeking a coastal European feel. Antalya offers a lush Mediterranean garden environment perfect for deeper immersion. Phuket is our tropical Asian flagship, combining jungle tranquility with world-class facilities. Sharm El Sheikh blends Red Sea views with warm Egyptian hospitality. St. Lucia is a boutique Caribbean experience surrounded by rainforest.

We recommend speaking to one of our wellness consultants to match the right destination to your program goals and travel preferences.

Our medical team conducts a thorough pre-program assessment to ensure the program selected is appropriate for your current health status, fitness level, and goals. Beginners often start with our 7-day Gentle Detox or Green Juice Cleanse, which provide a powerful yet manageable introduction to the TheLifeCo experience.

English is spoken fluently by all medical staff and most wellness team members across all locations. In Turkey, Turkish and German are also widely spoken. In Phuket, Thai is supported alongside English. Our international guest base means we frequently have multilingual staff available, and we can arrange interpretation services where needed.

We offer a comprehensive range of programs organized across five core categories: Cleansing & Detox (including medically supervised water fasting, green juice cleanse, and master detox), Weight Loss & Management, Chronic Disease Recovery & Prevention (diabetes, cardiovascular, inflammation, liver detox, gut health), Healthy Aging & Longevity (cancer prevention support, hormonal longevity, beauty and body reshaping), and Mental Health & Stress Management.

Every program is customized for the individual guest following a detailed medical intake consultation.

Yes. All programs at TheLifeCo are designed and overseen by qualified medical doctors, clinical nutritionists, and wellness specialists. Guests receive a health assessment on arrival including blood work, body composition analysis, and a one-on-one consultation with a physician. During your stay, you will have regular check-ins with the medical team, and any existing medications or health conditions are actively managed throughout your program.

Programs range from 4 days to 21 days, with the most popular durations being 7, 10, and 14 days. We recommend a minimum of 7 days to experience meaningful physiological changes. For chronic conditions or deeper transformation goals, 14 or 21-day programs allow the body sufficient time to complete a thorough detox cycle, stabilize metabolic markers, and build new habits under supported conditions.

Our medically supervised water fasting program is one of our most powerful offerings. Guests consume only water, herbal teas, and specific supplements during the fast period, while the medical team monitors vital signs, blood glucose, electrolytes, and overall wellbeing through daily check-ups.

The body enters a state of ketosis and initiates autophagy — cellular repair processes that have significant benefits for inflammation, metabolic health, and longevity. Most guests report clarity of mind, reduced joint inflammation, and significant weight loss during fasting programs. The re-feeding phase is carefully managed to ensure a safe and effective transition back to solid foods.

You can add optional supplementary therapies (hyperbaric oxygen, infrared sauna, colon hydrotherapy, specialized massages, yoga, or meditation sessions) to complement your core program. Our team will advise which additions are most appropriate for your goals.

Yes — proper preparation significantly enhances your results. After booking, you will receive a pre-arrival preparation guide from our team. A pre-program consultation call with our wellness team is included in all bookings to discuss your health history, goals, and what to expect.

All programs include full accommodation in your chosen room category, all program meals and juices (or water, in fasting programs), daily medical consultations, blood work analysis, body composition testing, scheduled treatment sessions (massage, therapy, etc.), group wellness classes (yoga, breathwork, guided meditation), and access to all resort facilities including pools, saunas, and fitness areas.

Optional add-on treatments, excursions, and premium room upgrades are available at an additional cost.

Room options vary by location but typically include Standard Rooms, Deluxe Rooms with garden or sea views, Junior Suites, and Premium Suites. All rooms are designed for rest and recovery — minimal distractions, quality beds, blackout curtains, and a calm natural aesthetic. Each location has a slightly different architecture, from the Aegean-inspired villas of Bodrum to the tropical garden bungalows of Phuket.

Yes, Wi-Fi is available throughout all resorts. You are welcome to bring your phone, laptop, and any personal devices. That said, we encourage guests to limit screen time during their stay, particularly in the first few days. Disconnecting from digital stress is an important part of the mental wellness benefit of your program, and many guests find a "digital detox" to be one of the most restorative aspects of their visit.

You can submit a booking request through our online Booking page or speak directly with one of our wellness consultants by phone or email. We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance to secure your preferred dates and room type, especially during peak seasons (June–August and January–March).

After your booking request is received, our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm availability, discuss your health goals, and finalize your program.

We understand that life circumstances can change. Our flexible cancellation policy allows full refunds for cancellations made more than 30 days before the arrival date. Cancellations between 15–29 days prior receive a 50% refund or full credit toward a future booking. Cancellations within 14 days of arrival are non-refundable but may be converted to a future booking credit at our discretion. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover unforeseen cancellations.

Pricing varies by resort location, room category, program type, and duration. As a general guide, a 7-day program in a standard room begins at approximately €1,900 per person at our Turkey locations and from €2,400 at our international locations. All-inclusive 14-day programs in premium suites can range up to €8,000+. We offer early booking discounts, seasonal promotions, and corporate rates for group bookings. Contact our team for a personalized quote.

Yes. We offer a deposit-based booking structure where a 30% deposit secures your reservation, with the balance payable 14 days before arrival. For longer programs, we can discuss installment arrangements on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our reservations team to discuss payment options that work for you.

Airport transfers are not included in the standard program price but can be arranged at an additional cost. We work with trusted local transfer partners at each location and can coordinate private vehicle transfers, shared shuttles, or business-class options depending on your preference.
