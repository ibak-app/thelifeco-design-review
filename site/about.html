Our Story

Founded on a
Vision of Healing

In 2005, Ali Yilmaz founded TheLifeCo with a bold belief: that lasting health transformation requires a medically supervised, holistic approach — not quick fixes. Starting from a single center in Turkey, the vision was to create retreats where science and nature work in unison.

Two decades later, TheLifeCo has grown into a global wellness leader operating 6 destinations across Turkey, Thailand, Egypt, and St. Lucia. Each center is built on the same founding principles — clinical expertise, personalised care, and a genuine commitment to long-term wellbeing.

To date, more than 40,000 guests from around the world have experienced lasting transformation at TheLifeCo, from professional athletes and executives to individuals seeking recovery and renewal.

Our Approach

The 360° Holistic
Method

Our programmes combine medical supervision, precision nutrition, physical fitness, mental wellness, and restorative spa therapies — five pillars working together for transformational results.

Medical Supervision

Board-certified physicians oversee every programme with clinical precision and evidence-based protocols.

Precision Nutrition

Plant-based, therapeutic nutrition plans crafted by specialist dietitians to accelerate detox and restore vitality.

Physical Fitness

Personalised movement therapies — yoga, pilates, aqua fitness, and functional training — designed for every level.

Mental Wellness

Mindfulness practices, guided meditation, and psychological support to build resilience and emotional clarity.

Spa Therapies

Ancient healing modalities and modern therapeutic treatments to restore the body and calm the nervous system.
