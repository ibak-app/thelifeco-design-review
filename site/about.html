Our Story

Two decades of transforming lives through science-backed wellness programs at five of the world's most breathtaking destinations.

Founded 2005

A Vision to Heal the World

In 2005, Ersin Pamuksüzer founded TheLifeCo with a singular vision: to create spaces where science and nature converge to restore human health and happiness. What started as a pioneering wellness center nestled in the hills above Bodrum, Turkey has grown into a global network of five luxury resort destinations, each unique in setting yet unified in philosophy.

Our approach was radical for its time — integrating naturopathic medicine, cellular-level detoxification, and raw-vegan nutrition under one roof, supported by a team of physicians, nutritionists, and wellness coaches. Today, after welcoming over 30,000 guests from 80 countries, the mission has only deepened.

The Four Pillars of Wellbeing

Every TheLifeCo program is built on four interconnected pillars that address the whole person — body, mind, and spirit.

Nutrition

Raw-vegan cuisine, cold-pressed juices, and personalized meal plans crafted by our in-house nutritionists to nourish at the cellular level.

Physical Activity

Yoga, Pilates, swimming, coastal hiking, and personalized fitness plans tailored to your current health status and goals.

Mental Wellness

Guided meditation, breathwork, one-on-one counseling, and structured stress management protocols for lasting mental clarity.

Inner Peace

Mindfulness practices, spiritual workshops, sound healing, and deep nature immersion to restore harmony and purpose.

Global Expansion

From Bodrum to the World

After establishing TheLifeCo as Turkey's most celebrated wellness destination, the brand expanded internationally — bringing its signature blend of medical expertise and holistic living to Phuket's tropical shores, the Egyptian Red Sea coast, and the lush rainforests of St. Lucia.

Each new location is chosen for its natural healing energy. Whether you are gazing at the Aegean from a Bodrum terrace or breathing in the salt air of the Caribbean, the environment itself becomes part of your treatment.
