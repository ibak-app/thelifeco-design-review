Step 2

Booking Questions

Everything you need to know before making your reservation.

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance to ensure your preferred resort, program, and dates are available. For peak seasons (May–September and December–January), we suggest booking 2–3 months ahead. Last-minute availability is sometimes possible — contact us directly for urgent requests.
Yes, a 30% deposit is required to secure your reservation. The remaining balance is due 14 days before your arrival. We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. Full payment plans are available for longer stays — ask your consultant for details.
Cancellations made 30+ days before arrival receive a full deposit refund. Cancellations 14–30 days before arrival receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 14 days of arrival are non-refundable, but we will work with you to reschedule at no extra charge in case of medical emergencies. Travel insurance is strongly recommended.
